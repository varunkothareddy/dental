Meet Dr. Dental

With 7 years of dedicated experience in advanced dentistry, Dr. Dental leads our clinic with a passion for precision and patient well-being. He holds a Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) in Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics, specializing in saving natural teeth through procedures like microscopic root canals.

Dr. Dental's philosophy is simple: combine modern technology with a gentle, personal touch. He is committed to providing the highest standard of dental care to the Ongole community, ensuring every patient feels comfortable, informed, and confident in their treatment.
