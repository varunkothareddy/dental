What is a Root Canal Treatment?

A Root Canal Treatment (RCT) is a dental procedure designed to save a tooth that is severely decayed or infected. This issue arises when the pulp—the soft tissue inside the tooth containing nerves and blood vessels—becomes inflamed or infected. If left untreated, it can cause severe pain, lead to an abscess, and eventually result in tooth loss. The goal of an RCT is to remove the damaged pulp, clean and disinfect the inside of the tooth, and then fill and seal it to prevent further infection.

Our Advanced Approach to Solving Root Canal Issues

At Dental Clinic, we redefine the root canal experience. We utilize cutting-edge technology, including high-magnification dental microscopes, to ensure unparalleled precision. This allows Dr. Bharath, an MDS in Endodontics, to see intricate details inside the tooth, leading to a more thorough and effective treatment. Combined with digital X-rays for minimal radiation and advanced rotary tools, we perform the procedure efficiently and, most importantly, painlessly. Our focus is on preserving your natural tooth while ensuring your complete comfort from start to finish.
